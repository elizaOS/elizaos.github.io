### Financial Infrastructure & Payment Gateways
A major focus this week was providing agents with robust tools for handling value exchange and financial security.
*   **Transaction Guardrails:** Integration of `@proofgate/eliza-plugin` provides essential validation guardrails for agent transactions ([#254](https://github.com/elizaos-plugins/registry/pull/254), [#257](https://github.com/elizaos-plugins/registry/pull/257)).

### Trading & Marketplace Capabilities
The registry expanded its support for decentralized commerce and advanced trading mechanics.
*   **Perpetual Trading:** The Arthur DEX plugin was added to support perpetual trading on the Orderly Network ([#256](https://github.com/elizaos-plugins/registry/pull/256)).
*   **Agent Economy:** The `plugin-moltbazaar` was integrated to facilitate an AI Agent Job Marketplace on Base, allowing agents to participate in a decentralized labor economy ([#255](https://github.com/elizaos-plugins/registry/pull/255)).

### Data Integration & Model Routing
New plugins were added to enhance the "intelligence" and information access of ElizaOS agents.
*   **Real-time Data:** Integration of `@elizaos/plugin-sportradar` allows agents to consume live sports data ([#250](https://github.com/elizaos-plugins/registry/pull/250)).
*   **Model Access:** The `@zane-archer/plugin-aimo-router` was added to streamline access to models from the AiMo Network ([#247](https://github.com/elizaos-plugins/registry/pull/247)).
