# elizaos-plugins/plugin-evm Daily Update (Feb 8, 2026)
## OVERVIEW 
Today's development focused on significant enhancements to the EVM plugin, including the integration of a multi-provider RPC abstraction and a comprehensive overhaul of the EVMService for improved wallet operations and chain interactions.

## KEY TECHNICAL DEVELOPMENTS

### Enhanced RPC Provider Flexibility
A new multi-provider RPC abstraction has been implemented, supporting Alchemy, Infura, Ankr, and Eliza Cloud proxy. This feature allows for selecting a preferred provider with automatic fallback, significantly improving reliability and chain coverage. ([#25](https://github.com/elizaos-plugins/plugin-evm/pull/25))

### Comprehensive EVM Service Overhaul
The EVMService has undergone a major overhaul, integrating with the Spartan-Intel chain registry. This update introduces robust wallet and key operations, validation and signing capabilities, balance and portfolio management, transfer functionalities, a DEX registry, and multicall batching, alongside provider optimizations. ([#24](https://github.com/elizaos-plugins/plugin-evm/pull/24))
