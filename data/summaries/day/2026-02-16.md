On Feb 16, 2026, the ElizaOS project saw significant progress in core feature development and integration enhancements within the `eliza` repository, with numerous issues closed related to integrations, bug fixes, and strategic initiatives. Concurrently, the `plugin-n8n-workflow` repository introduced comprehensive REST API routes for workflow management, while `registry` and `elizaos.github.io` focused on CI/CD improvements and dependency updates, respectively. The team is also actively preparing for the upcoming beta launch and refining Eliza's core personality.
