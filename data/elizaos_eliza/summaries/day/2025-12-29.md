# elizaos/eliza Daily Update (Dec 29, 2025)
## OVERVIEW 
Today's development focused on critical bug fixes, including standardizing server route naming and replacing process execution libraries. Documentation was also enhanced, and new issues were opened to address UI/UX improvements and advanced TypeScript patterns.
